Key Benefits

  • Natural Exfoliation: Gently removes dead skin cells and unclogs pores for a smoother, brighter complexion.
  • Deep Hydration: Infused with coconut and olive oils to nourish and moisturize even the driest skin.

Ingredients

  • Green Gram (Mung Bean): Natural exfoliant and brightening agent.
  • Coconut Oil: Deeply moisturizes and protects the skin barrier.
  • Olive Oil: Packed with vitamins A and E for nourishment and protection.

How to Use

  1. Wet your skin with lukewarm water.
  2. Lather the soap between your hands or using a soft cloth.
  3. Gently massage over your face and body in circular motions.
  4. Rinse thoroughly and pat dry with a clean towel.
  5. Use daily for best results.
